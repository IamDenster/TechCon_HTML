History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of engineers and entrepreneurs passionate about innovation and technology. Over the years, it has evolved into a globally recognized conference that celebrates advancements in artificial intelligence, robotics, and sustainable technology.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in technology. Our goal is to empower creators, developers, and thinkers to build technologies that drive positive global change. We aim to connect diverse voices in tech and inspire the next generation of innovators.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted an incredible lineup of speakers who have shaped the technology landscape.

Dr. Elena Rodriguez

A leading researcher in artificial intelligence ethics, Dr. Rodriguez shared insights on responsible AI development and the importance of inclusive data practices during her keynote in 2022.

Raj Patel

Founder of GreenTech Innovations, Raj Patel discussed sustainable technology and how renewable energy solutions can shape the future of smart cities.

Lisa Wong

As a renowned cybersecurity specialist, Lisa Wong emphasized the importance of data protection and the evolving landscape of online security in the digital era.
